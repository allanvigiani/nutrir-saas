## Anti-Patterns Verdict

LLM assessment: Uppercase tracking label reflex on 20+ elements. font-black at every typographic level. Detector: clean (no structural bans). Browser: unavailable.

## Overall Impression

Mechanically functional (food autocomplete + macro calculation is strong), but a critical trust gap: "Sincronizado em tempo real" with no autosave + silent data loss on ArrowLeft. Fix data safety first.

## What's Working

1. Food autocomplete with auto-calculation of macros including serving-weight conversion.
2. Empty state is useful and action-oriented.
3. Sidebar macro tracker with real-time progress against goals.

## Priority Issues

### [P1] Silent data loss on navigation
ArrowLeft → onClose → navigate() with no guard. 30 minutes of work lost in one misclick. Fix: useBlocker or localStorage draft autosave.

### [P1] "Sincronizado em tempo real" is misleading
Sidebar footer shows pulsing green dot and sync label. Nothing autosaves. Fix: real autosave + last-saved timestamp, or remove and replace with unsaved-changes badge.

### [P1] Save button label wrong for new plans
Always "Salvar Alterações" even on planId === 'new'. Fix: conditionally render "Criar Plano" vs "Salvar Alterações".

### [P2] Uppercase tracking label reflex everywhere
20+ instances of text-[11px] font-black uppercase tracking-[0.2em]. Breaks design system Label spec (font-medium, not font-black). Fix: reserve uppercase-tracked style for one navigation tier only.

### [P2] Corner radius vocabulary incoherent
7 distinct values including rounded-[1.5rem], rounded-[2rem], rounded-3xl — none in design system tokens. Fix: two tiers only (rounded-xl for content cards, rounded-2xl for sections).

### [P2] Malformed Tailwind classes
bg-primary/100, bg-primary/8, border-primary/20/50 (double-slash invalid). Fix specific values noted in critique.
